Frequently asked questions

Something is not clear? You need help? Check our FAQ section.

  • What are my payment options?

    You currently have three payment options to choose from: Free Plan – This option gives you basic access at no cost. It’s a great way to explore the service and use essential features. Free version gives you an access to two operators. Standard Plan ($5/month) – This plan offers additional features and improved performance compared to the free version. It’s ideal if you need a bit more flexibility and functionality. Standard version gives you an access to 5+ operators. Premium Plan ($10/month) – This is the most advanced option. It includes full access to all features, priority performance, and the best overall experience. Premium version gives you an access you 10+ operators.

  • How can I invite a collaborator to the platform?

    After creating your account you will have an opportunity to create your first project that will be associated with the collaborators e-mail. An invitation will be sent automatically. Then, invited user will have to confirm his account by e-mail and finish user onboarding.

  • Can I upgrade my plan?

    Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. Upgrading is quick and easy—you can switch to a higher plan whenever you need more features or better performance. The changes usually take effect immediately, so you can start enjoying the benefits of your new plan right away.

  • Can I cancel my plan at anytime?

    Yes, you can cancel your plan at any time. The cancellation process is simple and flexible, with no long-term commitments required. Once you cancel, your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period, so you can continue using all features until then.
